Who We Are

LVO Holdings is a private holding company rooted in Houston, Texas — built to own, operate, and steward assets across real estate, social infrastructure, and strategic capital ventures. We are not a firm that chases trends. We are a structure designed for permanence.

What We Stand For

II

Discretion By Design

We do not court attention. The work speaks through results — quietly, consistently, without announcement.

III

Through LVO Operations, we deploy resources into social services and community infrastructure. We believe ownership carries obligation.

Our Foundation
2026

Founded

LVO Holdings was established in Houston under the LVO Family Legacy Trust — structured from the outset as a permanent, multigenerational holding entity rather than a fund or venture vehicle.

Vindex

Real Estate

The first pillar — Vindex — was activated to acquire and develop properties positioned for generational appreciation. Protection of tangible assets is the bedrock of the structure.

Ops

Operations

LVO Operations channels capital into social services and community infrastructure — because durable wealth requires durable communities. Where purpose is not the byproduct of profit, but its precondition.

Alliance

Strategic Ventures

LVO Alliance exists for the rare relationship — partnerships forged on aligned conviction. Capital deployed here moves slowly, intentionally, and only with full confidence in the people behind it.
